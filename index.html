Campus Coronavirus Data

A tool for planning COVID-19 testing and vaccine distribution

This is a mapping tool intended to help higher-education institutions in Massachusetts understand the status of vaccine rollout across the state. In the map below, each blue dot corresponds to a college or university, sized according to the number of enrolled students. Hovering over a dot will display relevant information about the institution: MCVP status, partnerships with nearby medical centers, status as a vaccination site, and more. Currently, we can only display AICUM institutions, but we will expand the map as more data becomes available.

    Data sources

    Our enrollment and institutional data comes from MassGIS and the Integrated Postsecondary Education Data System (IPEDS). Our vaccine-related data is sourced from the Jan. 19, 2021 survey of AICUM colleges.
